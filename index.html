Investor framing

Consentext is positioning the gateway as trust infrastructure for health AI: the product layer that decides what can stay internal, what can be minimized, what can be exported, and what should never leave the user boundary.

Why it matters

It turns privacy from a product constraint into a routing advantage.

What is differentiated

Lane-based governance, reduced packets, and audit receipts in one product narrative.

What the demo proves

The system can make visible trust decisions, not just generate answers.

          Why this exists
          
          
          The gateway is a control system, not another AI feature
          
          
          Supporting context for after the live prototype run.
          
        
          
        
          
          
          
            
          Executive summary
          
            
          
              Consentext is trying to give users more than two bad choices: no AI at all, or direct disclosure into an
              external model. The AI Gateway sits between the Vault and AI systems so requests can be classified, routed,
              minimized, and audited.
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          What it does
          
            
            
              
          • Classifies the request.
            • 
              
          • Applies trust and exposure logic.
            • 
              
          • Routes the request to the right lane.
            • 
              
          • Preserves control with minimization and auditability.
            • 
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          What it is not
          
            
            
              
          • Not just a chatbot.
            • 
              
          • Not only a model router or API layer.
            • 
              
          • Not a final compliance architecture.
            • 
              
          • Not a replacement for model providers.
            • 
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          Best mental model
          
            
          The Vault stores.
          
            
          The Gateway governs.
          
            
          The AI reasons.
          
          
          
        
          
      
          

      
          
        
          
          
          System framing
          
          
          Consentext has three conceptual spaces
          
          
          Reference material for architecture and positioning questions.
          
        
          
        
          
          
          User
          
          
          →
          
          
          
            
          
              
          Vault
          
              
          User-owned health context: biomarkers, lab history, wearables, nutrition, fitness, uploaded records, and longitudinal patterns.
          
            
          
            
          
              
          Health Portal
          
              
          Clinical sharing and coordination with doctors, clinics, hospitals, and health systems. Separate from AI usage.
          
            
          
            
          
              
          AI Gateway
          
              
          The governed layer that decides how AI can interact with health context without sacrificing control.
          
            
          
          
          
        
          
        
          
          
          Canonical flow
          
          
          User Request → Gateway Classification → Exposure Decision → Lane Selection → Processing → Response → Audit Record
          
        
          
      
          

      
          
        
          
          
          Exposure spectrum
          
          
          Four governed lanes plus one contrast state
          
          
          Best read after the audience has seen a real route selected in the prototype.
          
        
          
        
          
          Most private
          
          
          Most AI power
        
          
        
          
          
          
            
          Lane 1
          
            
          Deterministic
          
            
          No AI. Structured retrieval, timelines, calculations, thresholds, missing-data checks, and templated summaries.
          
            
            
              
          • Provider: `none` / deterministic engine
            • 
              
          • Export: no external export
            • 
              
          • Role: trust anchor
            • 
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          Lane 2
          
            
          Private
          
            
          Protected AI explanation inside Consentext. Personal labs, records, and patterns stay inside the boundary.
          
            
            
              
          • Provider: `Qwen` / protected internal AI
            • 
              
          • Export: no external export
            • 
              
          • Role: highest-privacy AI lane
            • 
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          Lane 3
          
            
          Private+
          
            
          External AI can be used only after the gateway generates a minimized packet with the minimum necessary context.
          
            
            
              
          • Provider: reduced-context external AI
            • 
              
          • Export: reduced packet only
            • 
              
          • Role: adoption bridge
            • 
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          Lane 4
          
            
          Max Intelligence
          
            
          Broader governed external AI for open-ended comparisons, research exploration, and stronger capability.
          
            
            
              
          • Provider: governed external AI
            • 
              
          • Export: broader governed context may be allowed
            • 
              
          • Role: preserves user freedom
            • 
            
          
          
          
          
          
            
          Contrast state
          
            
          Off-Board
          
            
          The user bypasses Consentext and goes directly to an external AI tool. No routing, no minimization, no audit.
          
            
            
              
          • Provider: direct external AI
            • 
              
          • Export: unmanaged disclosure
            • 
              
          • Role: strategic contrast case
            • 
            
          
          
          
        
          
      
          

      
          
        
          
          
          Routing canon
          
          
          Baseline rules, prompt bank, and audit model
          
          
          Reference layer for the routing logic and canonical prompts.
          
        
          
        
          
          
          
            
          Routing principles
          
            
            
              
          1. If structured internal logic is enough, use Deterministic.
            2. 
              
          2. If explaining sensitive personal context, use Private.
            3. 
              
          3. If general guidance can work from minimized data, use Private+.
            4. 
              
          4. If the request is comparative, broader, or research-oriented, use Max Intelligence.
            5. 
              
          5. If the user bypasses Consentext, classify it as Off-Board.
            6. 
            
          
            
          Tie-break rule: default toward the more private lane when a prompt is ambiguous.
          
          
          
          
          
            
          Canonical reduced packet
          
            
          
              
          Age: 54
          
              
          Sex: Male
          
              
          LDL: 168
          
              
          HDL: 40
          
              
          Triglycerides: 210
          
              
          Relevant pattern: elevated LDL and triglycerides

                    Audit schema
                    
            
                      
              
                    • Required: timestamp, request, task_category, selected_lane, data_scope, external_export, provider, notes.
                      • 
              
                    • Optional: export_packet_type, minimization_applied, trust_mode, alternate_lane_considered, classification_confidence.
                      • 
            
                    
          
                    
        
                    

        
                    
          
                    
            
                    Canonical audit example
                    
            
                    {
  "timestamp": "2026-03-10T09:10:00",
  "request": "What lifestyle changes might improve these lab results?",
  "task_category": "reduced-context guidance",
  "selected_lane": "Private+",
  "data_scope": "reduced context packet",
  "external_export": true,
  "provider": "external AI",
  "notes": "Minimum necessary packet generated by gateway"
}
                    
          
                    
          
                    
            
                    Alignment rules from the canon
                    
            
                      
              
                    • Use the exact lane names: Deterministic, Private, Private+, Max Intelligence, Off-Board.
                      • 
              
                    • Use Qwen as the internal-model example for Private.
                      • 
              
                    • Use the same audit fields across prototypes.
                      • 
              
                    • Use the shared prompt set for baseline testing.
                      • 
              
                    • Do not drift into alternate lane definitions unless explicitly testing one.
                      •

                    Objectives
                    
            
                      
              
                    • Make the gateway feel like a believable control layer.
                      • 
              
                    • Make routing logic inspectable and easy to reason about.
                      • 
              
                    • Show lane-specific data handling.
                      • 
              
                    • Anchor the Private lane with Qwen.
                      • 
              
                    • Expose choices so founders can react to them.
                      • 
            
                    
          
                    
          
                    
            
                    Must-have behavior
                    
            
                      
              
                    • Free-text request input.
                      • 
              
                    • Visible classification result and rationale.
                      • 
              
                    • Lane-specific execution behavior.
                      • 
              
                    • Data scope display.
                      • 
              
                    • Audit receipt on every interaction.
                      • 
              
                    • Off-board comparison state.
                      • 
            
                    
          
                    
          
                    
            
                    Nice-to-have behavior
                    
            
                      
              
                    • Rule inspector.
                      • 
              
                    • Editable routing table.
                      • 
              
                    • Recommended lane vs alternate lane comparison.
                      • 
              
                    • Context packet visualizer.
                      • 
              
                    • Reusable prompt test suite panel.
                      • 
            
                    
          
                    
          
                    
            
                    Suggested components
                    
            
                      
              
                    • Request input
                      • 
              
                    • Classifier module
                      • 
              
                    • Lane controller
                      • 
              
                    • Data scope panel
                      • 
              
                    • Response area
                      • 
              
                    • Audit receipt
                      • 
              
                    • Optional rule inspector
                      • 
            
                    
          
                    
          
                    
            
                    Success looks like
                    
            
                      
              
                    • The team can see why one prompt lands in Private and another in Private+.
                      • 
              
                    • Qwen feels concrete inside the protected lane.
                      • 
              
                    • Minimization and auditability feel real.
                      • 
              
                    • The off-board path sharpens the value of the gateway.
                      • 
              
                    • The concept becomes concrete enough to refine.
                      • 
            
                    
          
                    
          
                    
            
                    What to avoid
                    
            
                      
              
                    • Do not turn it into a prettier chatbot.
                      • 
              
                    • Do not collapse everything into one lane.
                      • 
              
                    • Do not overbuild final infrastructure.
                      • 
              
                    • Do not over-focus on compliance, vendor ranking, or final API stack yet.
                      • 
              
                    • Do not make it a diagnostic tool.
